Meet the Instructors/Mentors

Everyone who helped us!

Andrew Sachs, CEO

Andrew, a retired tech entrepreneur, and his teacher wife, Karen, started Nobel to help families. The online coaching and tutoring helps students succeed in school. Nobel Explorers, the online, international STEM camp, helps students master the hard skills, soft skills and global cultural awareness needed in thrive in life and the future of work.

Talent Experience Lead & Coach Community Manager

Hi, I'm Nadja. I'm a Talent Experience Lead & a Coach Community Manager here at Nobel, which pretty much means I try to take the best care possible of all the people working with Nobel. I help teams select the best talent possible, support all our contractors in their work, and communicate the needs of our coaches to the team & vice versa.
