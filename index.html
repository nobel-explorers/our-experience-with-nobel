We are the Nobel Explorers interns of 2019. Nobel Explorers is an organization founded by Andrew Sachs, with the mission to spread accessible STEM education to students around the world. We are a group of thirteen interns who are learning more about Nobel and helping it reach its goal. Not only do we learn various skills about coding and web design, but we also learn how to communicate and work with people in effective ways, including through the internet, due to the fact that team members are in different parts of the world. We hope you will want to intern at Nobel by looking at what else we have done which will be presented throughout the remainder of the website.
